Storyline and models

During the game you build an ancient civilization and other civilizations you fight the enemy .

Yamato

Yamato is the name of a Japanese dynasty that founded a state with a capital city at Fujiwara. The Yamato are the ancestors of modern Japanese people and were the first people to unify and conquer all of Japan. The architecture is characterized by its Asian style.

Hittites

The Hittites are based on a powerful Iron Age civilization once located in Anatolia with their capital situated at Hattusa in the 18th century B.C. They spoke a language from a now extinct branch of the Indo-European language family. The architecture is characterized by its Babylonian style.

Sumerian

Sumerian civilization took form in the Uruk period (4th millennium B.C.), continuing into the Jemdat Nasr and Early Dynastic periods. It was conquered by the Semitic-speaking kings of the Akkadian Empire around 2400 BC. The architecture is characterized by using stone as a material.

Persian

Persia refers to an ancient Middle-Eastern kingdom ruled by the inhabitants, known as the Persians which is primarily based on Achaemenid Empire located roughly in present day Iran. The architecture is characterized by its Babylonian style.

ENEMY AI

La Enemy AI, o inteligencia artificial del enemigo, se ha partido en 3 scripts principales:

IA: es la inteligencia principal. La que toma decisiones sobre qué hacer y cuándo.

CitizenAI: Inteligencia artificial del ciudadano. Obedece a AI a la hora de buscar recursos y recolectarlo. Mientras no reciba órdenes por parte de AI, recolectará indistintamente recursos que vaya encontrando.

ADS(Attack Decision System): Inteligencia artificial militar que se encarga de todo el sistema de decisión de acciones militares. Utiliza una estrategia muy básica que consiste en patrullar por el mapa mediante un movimiento aleatorio y un sistema de detección el cual es capaz de detectar todos los componentes del jugador (Player) dentro de su radio de detección. Una vez detectado el objetivo, se añade a una lista de objetivos la cual comparten todas las unidades militares permitiendo el ataque enemigo con todas las unidades de las que disponga sobre el mismo objetivo. En general, una estrategia básica pero que en según que situaciones puede ser efectiva.

CitizenAI: La Inteligencia artificial del Citizen (ubicada en el script CitizenAI.cs) se encarga de decidir dónde debe recolectar recursos i qué recurso debe recolectar (madera, comida u oro) dependiendo de las necesidades del momento. Para ello, una vez definido qué tipo de recurso es, se crea un objeto de tipo Resource a partir del collider del Hit que se genera al topar con el y se llama al método StartHarvest de CivilUnit pasándole como parámetro el tag del tipo de recurso que necesitemos en ese momento.

Las necesidades del momento serán determinadas por el script AI.cs que será el que decidirá si hay que construir o qué construir y qué recursos se necesitan para hacerlo. Para ello, utilizará los 3 posibles estados, modificando la variable AIState:
AIState = 1 -> Recolectará madera.
AIState = 2 -> Recolectará comida.
AIState = 3 -> Recolectará oro.
En el diagrama adjunto, se puede ver la relación que guardan el script CitizenAI.cs con los scripts CivilUnit.cs, Unit.cs y RTSObject.cs, éste último script principal adjunto en todos y cada uno de los objetos que están en el mapa.

The Game Controls

This section contains a simple manual of the features of the game that can be executed through keyboard or mouse. Some of these features allow game developers to observe the events that occur in the game and the other to control the game by the user.

Fog of War

1. Press ["R"] = When the user press "R", fog of war is disabled.
2. Press ["U"] = When the user press "U", fog of war is activated.

Buildings

1. Press ["K"] = When the user presses "K" all existing buildings on the map appear semi-demolished.

Game

1. Press ["ESC"] = Game Menu
2. Press ["M"] = Insta-kill enemies
3. Press ["P"] = Insta-kill player

A. I.

1. Press ["X"] = When the user press "X" prints out a table with information about the enemy. Resources, buildings and troops. [Activate/Deactivate]
2. Press ["G"+"↑"] = When the user clicks this combination, Gold Resources enemy increases by 1000 units.
3. Press ["G"+"↓"] = When the user clicks this combination, Gold Resources enemy decreases by 1000 units.
4. Press ["F"+"↑"] = When the user clicks this combination, Food Resources enemy increases by 1000 units.
5. Press ["F"+"↓"] = When the user clicks this combination, Food Resources enemy decreases by 1000 units.
6. Press ["W"+"↑"] = When the user clicks this combination, Wood Resources enemy increases by 1000 units.
7. Press ["W"+"↓"] = When the user clicks this combination, Wood Resources enemy decreases by 1000 units.

Camera

1. Press ["↑"], ["↓"], ["←"], ["→"], [Mouse] = Movement of the camera within the map. Place the mouse cursor to the screen borders to move the camera.
2. Press ["W"] = When the user press "W" the camera rotate up.
3. Press ["S"] = When the user press "S" the camera rotate down.
4. Press ["A"] = When the user press "A" the camera rotate left.
5. Press ["D"] = When the user press "D" the camera rotate right.
6. [Mouse + "CTRL"] = Rotate the camera with the movement of the mouse.

Who Are We

We are a group of young students who have developed this game over the entire tree months to carry out the practical side , the field of Software Engineering .

Our Process

For the entire game development process we used engineering software . To organize the project we used SCRUM , Agile methodologies within which management is an iterative process where requirements change rapidly .

Our Approach

We have done development using professional equipment for each sector. Each team has a scrum master who support us in the planning of the remaining tasks . With every sprint we improve our speed and quality of the projects.

Our Goal

Our goal is to implement new civilizations , many more features of the game and more portability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop a game and improving it continuously in function of the feedback from customers.
